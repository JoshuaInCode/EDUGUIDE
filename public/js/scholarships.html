UNIVERSITY SCHOLARSHIPS IN PANAMA

IFARHU Excellence Scholarship

Full Coverage Deadline: March 31st, 2024

Description

The IFARHU Excellence Scholarship provides full financial support for outstanding students pursuing undergraduate studies at Panama's top universities.

Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 4.0 (or equivalent)
  • Panamanian citizenship or permanent residency
  • High school diploma
  • Letter of acceptance from an accredited university
  • Demonstrated financial need
Coverage Includes:
  • Full tuition fees
  • Monthly stipend
  • Book allowance
  • Transportation allowance

Fulbright Scholarship Program

Full Coverage Deadline: May 15th, 2024

Description

The Fulbright Program offers opportunities for Panamanian students to pursue graduate studies in the United States through a comprehensive scholarship program.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree with excellent academic record
  • English proficiency (TOEFL score of 90+ or equivalent)
  • Panamanian citizenship
  • Two years of work experience (preferred)
  • Strong leadership potential
Coverage Includes:
  • Full tuition and fees
  • Monthly living stipend
  • Health insurance
  • Round-trip airfare

SENACYT Academic Excellence Scholarship

Partial Coverage Deadline: June 30th, 2024

Description

SENACYT offers scholarships for undergraduate and graduate studies in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields at top universities worldwide.

Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Focus on STEM fields
  • Panamanian citizenship
  • Acceptance letter from an accredited university
  • Research proposal (for graduate studies)
Coverage Includes:
  • Partial tuition coverage
  • Research allowance
  • Equipment funding
  • Conference attendance support
