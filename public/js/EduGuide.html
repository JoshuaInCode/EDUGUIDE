WHAT IS EDUGUIDE?

EduGuide was born from the idea of providing young people with a safe place where they can find validated information about university programs and scholarships, as there is a significant lack of comprehensive information when searching for programs and scholarships.

MISSION

To provide a centralized platform in Panama for people to discover universities and scholarships, ensuring equitable access to higher education resources and opportunities for all.

VISION

To empower every Panamanian to easily access higher education opportunities, regardless of their background, fostering personal and professional growth.

BENEFITS

We provide students with resources and support, helping them make informed decisions for a more inclusive higher education system.
